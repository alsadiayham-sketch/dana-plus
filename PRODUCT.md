# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Dana manages her product catalog, representatives, sales, payments, and delivery charges in Arabic from a desktop or mobile device.

## Product Purpose

Dana Plus keeps the operating data for sales and representative earnings in one synchronized workspace, with calculations that reflect completed orders.

## Brand Personality

Clear, trusted, and focused.

## Anti-references

Avoid sparse decorative dashboards, unclear financial states, and cramped mobile controls.

## Design Principles

- Keep operational data easy to scan.
- Make editable and calculated values distinct.
- Preserve a calm, consistent Arabic RTL workflow.
- Support fast daily actions without hiding key context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain legible Arabic text, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and 44px minimum mobile touch targets.
